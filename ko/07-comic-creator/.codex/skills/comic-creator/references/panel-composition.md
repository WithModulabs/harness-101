# Panel Composition

Use for storyboard and image-prompt decisions.

## 4-Panel Patterns

| Pattern | Structure | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Setup-development-twist-punchline | Equal 4-stack | Comedy, daily observation |
| Wide-close | Wide, standard, standard, close-up | Drama, emotion |
| Zoom in | Long shot, medium, near, close-up | Rising tension, discovery |
| Time jump | Past, present, present, result | Contrast, growth |

## Webtoon Patterns

| Technique | Layout | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Expanded whitespace | 2-3x gap between panels | Time passing, aftertaste |
| Full-width panel | Uses full screen width | Impact, transition |
| Tall vertical panel | Long-height panel | Falling, height, scale |
| Rapid sequence | 3-5 small panels | Fast action, tension |
| Silent panel | No dialogue | Emotion, reader interpretation |

## Camera Angles

| Angle | Emotional Effect | Prompt Keywords |
|---|---|---|
| Low angle | Power, authority, heroic scale | `low angle shot, looking up` |
| High angle | Weakness, isolation, overview | `high angle shot, bird's eye` |
| Eye level | Equality, objectivity | `eye level shot` |
| Dutch angle | Anxiety, confusion | `dutch angle, tilted frame` |
| Over shoulder | Dialogue tension, viewpoint | `over the shoulder shot` |
| POV | Immersion, identification | `POV shot, first person` |

## Panel Size and Time

- Small panel: short time, reaction, quick action.
- Large panel: long time, emotion, atmosphere.
- Borderless panel: suspended time, memory, dream.

## Reading Flow

- Korean horizontal text: speech bubbles generally flow top-to-bottom and left-to-right.
- Webtoon: top-to-bottom scroll; use whitespace as timing.
- Character gaze should guide the reader toward the next panel or bubble.
- Rightward movement often feels like progress; leftward movement can imply retreat or memory.

## Page Rhythm

| Rhythm | Panel Pattern | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Strong-weak-strong-weak | Large, small, large, small | Stable conversation |
| Build then hit | Small, small, small, large | Tension then impact |
| Strong then detail | Large, small, small, small | Transition then inspection |
| Even | Equal sizes | Daily rhythm |
| Zoom-in | Large to smaller panels | Focus, pressure |
| Zoom-out | Small to large panels | Scale expansion |

## Prompt Skeleton

```text
[art style], [shot size], [camera angle], [composition].
[character anchor phrase]. [action and expression].
[background/environment]. [lighting]. [emotion/mood].
[comic technique]. Text policy: [NO TEXT / Korean text exactly "..."].
```
